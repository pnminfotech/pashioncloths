Pashion Clothing Pvt. ltd is a custom apparel manufacturer and decorator company. We are a trusted partner in providing modern apparel design solutions for the corporate world. Established in 2013, we are well equipped with state of the art technology and skilled manpower. Our principle of delivering superior value to our customers and clients has set us apart from our competitors.

We are backed by a state of art infrastructure and facility which is equipped with ultra-modern machines and technologies in order to facilitate producing in bulk quantities and at faster production cycles. The facility deals with end-to-end operations (Sourcing, Design, Production, Sampling, and Dispatch) of units to customers resulting in smooth operations.

Pashion Innovate:Discovering Tomorrow's Trends Today

At Pashion Clothing Pvt. ltd, we creatively combine traditional and new age innovative techniques while manufacturing the unique range of products. Our strength lies in our ability to give our customers complete controlled facilities at all stages involved in the product development and production process, thereby ensuring innovativeness in design, quality in production and on time deliveries.

Our sewing unit is equipped With 20 Machines, collectively imported from Juki, Peqasus, Yamato, & Sirubabrands. Set up is backed by a skilled workforce to assure the consistent quality throughout the process. We believe that technology, speed and skilled labor, bring out the best quality products.
